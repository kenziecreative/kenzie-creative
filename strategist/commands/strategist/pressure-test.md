Pressure-test the current strategy.

Use the `strategist-pressure-test` skill and follow its steps. It reads the working
brief (or a specific stage you name), dispatches the `strategist-critic` agent to
stress-test the reasoning — unstated assumptions, logical gaps, weak inferences,
alternative framings, and failure modes — and returns the findings for you to address.

It interrogates the strategy; it does not rewrite it. Acting on the findings is your
call, made back in the relevant stage.
