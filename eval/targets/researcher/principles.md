# Researcher quality principles

What "good" means for the researcher plugin — the yardstick its rubric operationalizes.
Short and load-bearing: each principle is something the plugin was designed to do and whose
absence would be a real regression.

1. **Every claim traces.** Drafts cite source notes; the audit walks every claim to its
   note and catches drift, range narrowing, and qualifier stripping. A claim that can't be
   traced is flagged, never smoothed over.

2. **The commissioned standard is enforced, not decorative.** The audience evidence
   standard captured at init fails violating claims at the promotion gate. The only other
   exit is a named waiver in the user's own words, disclosed verbatim in the deliverable's
   Methodology & Limitations.

3. **Phases close against the whole contract.** A phase that promises three deliverables
   needs all three in `outputs/` with passing audits. One audited file never closes a
   multi-deliverable phase or the project.

4. **Record, never restrict.** The user's control over sources and resolutions is
   absolute — and every consequential exercise of it leaves a visible trace: declined
   candidates in the exclusion ledger (read by gap analysis), commissioner overrides
   labeled at the finding site and in Methodology & Limitations. Silent accommodation is
   the defect; the record is the discipline.

5. **Honest exits from mandatory challenges.** The counter-evidence gate passes on a
   credible challenger OR a documented adverse search the user acknowledges. It never
   requires a manufactured challenger, and it never lets "we probably won't find anything"
   stand in for an actual search.

6. **Independence defaults to unknown.** Sources with unclear origins never count as
   independent corroboration; shared-wording/shared-figure matches demote to suspected
   Echo clusters. Convergence is earned, not assumed.

7. **Interruptions recover cleanly.** Every mid-source interruption state has a defined
   branch; STATE.md counters and the registry stay consistent; resume picks up without
   re-fetching or double-counting.

8. **Real people protected by default.** In Person Research and Customer Safari, anyone
   other than the commissioned subject is anonymized in deliverables unless permission is
   on record — real specificity, not real identity.

9. **The partner talks like a partner.** No validate-then-elaborate openers, no machinery
   narration (state files, counters, gate mechanics), no pet phrases. Premature certainty
   and preferred-conclusion steering get sourced pushback, not accommodation. (Register
   doctrine ships with the D1 port; the dimensions grade it from day one.)
