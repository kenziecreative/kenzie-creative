# Lenses Reference

This file defines lens options for Nano Banana Pro simulation. The lens determines compression, bokeh (background blur), and overall "feel" of the image.

---

## Understanding Focal Length

- **Wide (14-35mm):** Expansive, dramatic, shows environment
- **Standard (40-60mm):** Natural, human-eye perspective
- **Portrait (70-105mm):** Flattering compression, subject isolation
- **Telephoto (135mm+):** Strong compression, dramatic background separation

---

## Portrait & Fashion Lenses

### 85mm f/1.2
- **Type:** Portrait Prime
- **Bokeh:** Extremely creamy, circular
- **Best For:** Headshots, fashion, beauty
- **Effect:** Strong subject separation, flattering facial compression
- **Background:** Melts away into smooth blur
- **Use When:** Single subject portraits; need maximum background blur; beauty shots

### 85mm f/1.4
- **Type:** Portrait Prime
- **Bokeh:** Very smooth, slightly more defined than f/1.2
- **Best For:** Portraits, events, environmental portraits
- **Effect:** Classic portrait compression with excellent sharpness
- **Use When:** Portraits where you want sharp eyes with smooth background

### 50mm f/1.2
- **Type:** Standard Prime
- **Bokeh:** Creamy, natural
- **Best For:** Environmental portraits, lifestyle, documentary
- **Effect:** Natural perspective, intimate feel
- **Background:** Soft but with some context visible
- **Use When:** Want to show some environment; lifestyle portraits; storytelling

### 50mm f/1.4
- **Type:** Standard Prime
- **Bokeh:** Smooth, classic
- **Best For:** All-around, street portraits, events
- **Effect:** Most "natural" looking perspective
- **Use When:** General purpose; want images that feel authentic

### 35mm f/1.4
- **Type:** Wide Prime
- **Bokeh:** Smooth with environmental context
- **Best For:** Environmental portraits, street, lifestyle
- **Effect:** Shows subject in their world, slight dramatic feel
- **Use When:** Want to include surroundings; storytelling; documentary style

### 105mm f/1.4
- **Type:** Portrait Telephoto
- **Bokeh:** Extremely smooth, strong compression
- **Best For:** Beauty, fashion, headshots with distance
- **Effect:** Maximum facial flattering, very shallow depth
- **Use When:** Beauty photography; want extreme subject isolation

## Wide Angle Lenses

### 16-35mm f/2.8
- **Type:** Wide Zoom
- **Bokeh:** Minimal (wide angles have deeper depth of field)
- **Best For:** Architecture, interiors, landscape, real estate
- **Effect:** Expansive, dramatic, shows full environment
- **Distortion:** Can distort at edges; AI will correct
- **Use When:** Interiors; architecture; want to show scale

### 14mm f/1.8
- **Type:** Ultra-Wide Prime
- **Bokeh:** Very deep depth of field
- **Best For:** Dramatic landscapes, astrophotography, architecture
- **Effect:** Extreme perspective, dramatic leading lines
- **Use When:** Night sky; dramatic architecture; environmental shots

### 24mm f/1.4
- **Type:** Wide Prime
- **Bokeh:** Moderate for a wide lens
- **Best For:** Environmental portraits, photojournalism, travel
- **Effect:** Immersive, "you are there" feeling
- **Use When:** Travel; documentary; want subject in environment

### 20mm f/1.8
- **Type:** Wide Prime
- **Bokeh:** Deep but usable
- **Best For:** Interior, landscape, group shots
- **Effect:** Natural wide perspective without extreme distortion
- **Use When:** Real estate; group photos; landscape with foreground interest

---

## Macro Lenses

### 90mm Macro f/2.8
- **Type:** Macro Prime
- **Bokeh:** Smooth, controlled
- **Best For:** Jewelry, watches, small products, food details
- **Effect:** Extreme close-up detail, shows textures and reflections
- **Minimum Focus:** Very close, captures tiny details
- **Use When:** Product detail shots; jewelry; food close-ups; textures

### 100mm Macro f/2.8
- **Type:** Macro Prime
- **Bokeh:** Creamy, excellent subject isolation
- **Best For:** Products, beauty details, nature close-ups
- **Effect:** 1:1 reproduction, excellent working distance
- **Use When:** Need distance from subject while capturing detail

### 50mm Macro f/2.8
- **Type:** Compact Macro
- **Bokeh:** Moderate
- **Best For:** Food, small products, tabletop
- **Effect:** Natural perspective macro
- **Use When:** Food photography; tabletop products

---

## Telephoto Lenses

### 70-200mm f/2.8
- **Type:** Telephoto Zoom
- **Bokeh:** Very smooth, professional
- **Best For:** Events, sports, portraits with distance, wildlife
- **Effect:** Versatile compression, strong background separation
- **Use When:** Events; need flexibility; sports; distant subjects

### 135mm f/1.8
- **Type:** Portrait Telephoto
- **Bokeh:** Extremely creamy
- **Best For:** Fashion, portraits, performances
- **Effect:** Strong compression, dreamy background
- **Use When:** Fashion editorials; outdoor portraits; concerts

### 200mm f/2.0
- **Type:** Fast Telephoto
- **Bokeh:** Maximum blur at telephoto range
- **Best For:** Sports, wildlife, fashion runway
- **Effect:** Dramatic compression, complete background elimination
- **Use When:** Professional sports; wildlife; runway fashion

## Specialty Lenses

- **Type:** Portrait Tilt-Shift

- **Use When:** Fine art portraits; creative editorial

## Aperture Guide

| Aperture | Depth of Field | Best For |
|----------|---------------|----------|
| f/1.2 - f/1.4 | Extremely shallow | Single subject, dreamy backgrounds |
| f/1.8 - f/2.0 | Very shallow | Portraits with slight context |
| f/2.8 | Shallow | Products, group portraits |
| f/4 | Moderate | Products needing more in focus |
| f/5.6 - f/8 | Deep | Products, food, architecture |
| f/11 - f/16 | Very deep | Landscape, interiors |
