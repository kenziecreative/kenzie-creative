# Lighting Styles Reference

This file defines lighting setups for Nano Banana Pro simulation. Lighting is where the reasoning engine "re-renders" the 3D space to create professional results.

---

## Understanding Light Quality

- **Hard Light:** Creates defined shadows, dramatic, edgy
- **Soft Light:** Gradual shadow transitions, flattering, gentle
- **Directional:** Light from specific angle, creates dimension
- **Diffused:** Scattered light, even illumination

---

## Portrait Lighting Setups

### Rembrandt Lighting
- **Description:** Named after the painter; creates a small triangle of light on the shadowed cheek
- **Mood:** Classic, dramatic, sophisticated, serious
- **Key Light Position:** 45° to side, slightly above subject
- **Shadow Pattern:** One side of face in shadow with triangle highlight
- **Best For:** Corporate headshots, artistic portraits, dramatic mood
- **Modifier:** Medium softbox or beauty dish at angle
- **Use When:** Want timeless, painterly quality; serious subjects; editorial

### Butterfly Lighting (Paramount)
- **Description:** Light directly above and in front of subject, creates butterfly-shaped shadow under nose
- **Mood:** Glamorous, beauty, high-fashion
- **Key Light Position:** Directly in front, above eye level
- **Shadow Pattern:** Shadow under nose and chin, even illumination on cheeks
- **Best For:** Beauty photography, fashion, glamour, skincare
- **Modifier:** Large softbox or beauty dish overhead
- **Use When:** Beauty shots; need to minimize skin texture; glamour

### Loop Lighting
- **Description:** Light slightly to side, creates small shadow of nose on cheek (not touching)
- **Mood:** Friendly, approachable, natural
- **Key Light Position:** 30-45° to side, slightly above
- **Shadow Pattern:** Soft shadow loops from nose toward cheek
- **Best For:** General portraits, headshots, natural look
- **Modifier:** Large softbox
- **Use When:** Want flattering but not dramatic; everyday portraits

### Split Lighting
- **Description:** Half the face lit, half in shadow
- **Mood:** Dramatic, moody, mysterious, edgy
- **Key Light Position:** 90° to side
- **Shadow Pattern:** Exactly half face illuminated
- **Best For:** Artistic portraits, dramatic effect, character shots
- **Modifier:** Any hard or soft source from side
- **Use When:** Maximum drama; artistic expression; moody portraits

### Broad Lighting
- **Description:** Light falls on the side of face turned toward camera
- **Mood:** Open, accessible, can widen narrow faces
- **Key Light Position:** Lights the broad side of turned face
- **Shadow Pattern:** Shadow falls on far side from camera
- **Best For:** Subjects with narrow faces, casual portraits
- **Use When:** Want to add visual width; casual feel

### Short Lighting
- **Description:** Light falls on the side of face turned away from camera
- **Mood:** Slimming, dramatic, sophisticated
- **Key Light Position:** Lights the short side of turned face
- **Shadow Pattern:** Shadow on side closest to camera
- **Best For:** Slimming effect, adding dimension, professional headshots
- **Use When:** Want to slim face; add depth; professional look

## Studio Setups

### Three-Point Lighting
- **Description:** Classic setup with key, fill, and back/rim light
- **Components:**
  - **Key Light:** Main light, 45° to side
  - **Fill Light:** Softer light opposite key, reduces shadows
  - **Back/Rim Light:** Behind subject, creates edge separation
- **Mood:** Professional, clean, commercial
- **Best For:** Commercial photography, products, corporate
- **Use When:** Need clean, professional, balanced illumination

### High-Key Lighting
- **Description:** Bright, even lighting with minimal shadows
- **Mood:** Clean, airy, optimistic, pure, minimal
- **Setup:** Multiple soft lights, white background, fill from all angles
- **Shadow Pattern:** Almost no shadows, very flat
- **Best For:** Beauty, skincare, tech products, minimalist aesthetic
- **Use When:** Apple-style aesthetic; skincare; bright commercial look

### Low-Key Lighting
- **Description:** Predominantly dark with selective highlights
- **Mood:** Dramatic, mysterious, moody, cinematic
- **Setup:** Single hard or controlled soft light, dark background
- **Shadow Pattern:** Deep shadows, selective illumination
- **Best For:** Dramatic portraits, fine art, moody products
- **Use When:** Maximum drama; film noir aesthetic; luxury dark

### Clamshell Lighting
- **Description:** Two lights - one above, one below (like a clamshell)
- **Mood:** Beauty, glamorous, even, flattering
- **Setup:** Main light above, reflector or fill below chin
- **Shadow Pattern:** Minimal, fills under chin and eye sockets
- **Best For:** Beauty, fashion, headshots
- **Use When:** Beauty photography; need very flattering light

## Natural Light Styles

### Golden Hour
- **Description:** Warm, soft light during sunrise/sunset
- **Mood:** Romantic, warm, dreamy, nostalgic
- **Light Quality:** Soft, directional, warm color temperature (3000-4000K)
- **Shadow Pattern:** Long, soft shadows with warm fill
- **Best For:** Portraits, lifestyle, travel, romantic scenes
- **Use When:** Want warmth; romance; outdoor lifestyle

### Blue Hour
- **Description:** Cool, diffused light just before sunrise/after sunset
- **Mood:** Serene, calm, mysterious, cinematic
- **Light Quality:** Very soft, cool color temperature (6000-10000K)
- **Shadow Pattern:** Almost shadowless, even illumination
- **Best For:** Cityscapes, moody portraits, architecture
- **Use When:** Want cool, serene mood; city shots; twilight feel

### Overcast/Cloudy
- **Description:** Soft, even light filtered through clouds
- **Mood:** Neutral, natural, documentary
- **Light Quality:** Very soft, no harsh shadows
- **Shadow Pattern:** Minimal, soft gradients
- **Best For:** Portraits (nature's softbox), products outdoors
- **Use When:** Want even, flattering natural light; outdoor portraits

### Harsh Midday Sun
- **Description:** Direct overhead sunlight
- **Mood:** Bold, contrasty, dramatic, raw
- **Light Quality:** Very hard, creates strong shadows
- **Shadow Pattern:** Deep, defined shadows under features
- **Best For:** Fashion editorial, dramatic effect, bold statements
- **Use When:** Want bold graphic shadows; fashion; intentional harshness

### Window Light
- **Description:** Soft directional light from large window
- **Mood:** Natural, intimate, authentic
- **Light Quality:** Soft but directional, beautiful gradients
- **Shadow Pattern:** Soft falloff from light to shadow
- **Best For:** Portraits, lifestyle, product photography
- **Use When:** Want natural, authentic feel; indoor lifestyle

### Open Shade
- **Description:** Subject in shade with open sky providing fill
- **Mood:** Natural, even, outdoor professional
- **Light Quality:** Soft, slightly cool
- **Shadow Pattern:** Very soft, minimal contrast
- **Best For:** Outdoor portraits, headshots
- **Use When:** Outdoor portraits; need even lighting outside

## Product & Commercial Lighting

### Top-Down Softbox
- **Description:** Large soft light directly overhead
- **Mood:** Clean, commercial, editorial
- **Setup:** Large softbox suspended above product
- **Shadow Pattern:** Shadow directly below, even top illumination
- **Best For:** Food, flat-lay, products from above
- **Use When:** Flat-lay photography; food; overhead shots

### Rim Lighting (Edge Light)
- **Description:** Light from behind creates glowing edge
- **Mood:** Dramatic, premium, separation
- **Setup:** Light(s) behind subject pointing toward camera
- **Shadow Pattern:** Subject mostly dark with glowing edges
- **Best For:** Silhouettes, product separation, dramatic portraits
- **Use When:** Need subject separation; dramatic edge; premium feel

### Gradient Background Lighting
- **Description:** Background lit to create smooth gradient
- **Mood:** Professional, studio, premium
- **Setup:** Lights on background creating light-to-dark sweep
- **Shadow Pattern:** N/A - affects background only
- **Best For:** Products, headshots, commercial
- **Use When:** Want professional studio background

### Light Painting
- **Description:** Multiple exposures or moving light for even product coverage
- **Mood:** Premium, luxury, high-end commercial
- **Setup:** Complex multi-light or painted light technique
- **Shadow Pattern:** Controlled, often minimal
- **Best For:** Cars, luxury products, reflective surfaces
- **Use When:** High-end commercial; reflective products

### Strip Light Setup
- **Description:** Narrow strip softboxes for controlled highlights
- **Mood:** Fashion, edgy, defined
- **Setup:** Strip boxes on sides creating long highlights
- **Shadow Pattern:** Defined edges, dramatic falloff
- **Best For:** Fashion, bottles, cylindrical products
- **Use When:** Need long highlight streaks; bottles; fashion

## Cinematic Lighting

### Teal and Orange
- **Description:** Complementary color lighting scheme
- **Mood:** Cinematic, Hollywood, dramatic
- **Setup:** Warm key light, cool fill or background
- **Use When:** Want movie-poster feel; dramatic color contrast

### Practical Lighting
- **Description:** Using visible light sources in frame (lamps, neon, etc.)
- **Mood:** Atmospheric, authentic, narrative
- **Use When:** Want environmental storytelling; authentic scene

### Motivated Lighting
- **Description:** Studio lighting that mimics natural source in scene
- **Mood:** Natural but controlled
- **Use When:** Need studio control with natural appearance

## Quick Reference by Scene Type

| Scene Type | Recommended Lighting | Why |
|------------|---------------------|-----|
| Corporate Headshot | Rembrandt or Loop | Professional, flattering, serious |
| Beauty/Skincare | Butterfly or Clamshell | Even, glamorous, minimizes texture |
| Fashion Editorial | Split or Harsh Sun | Dramatic, edgy, bold |
| Product Commercial | Three-Point or High-Key | Clean, professional, detailed |
| Food | Top-Down Softbox + Side Fill | Shows texture, appetizing |
| Lifestyle | Golden Hour or Window Light | Natural, authentic, warm |
| Real Estate Interior | Window Light + Flash Fill | Natural with detail in shadows |
| Dramatic Portrait | Low-Key or Rembrandt | Moody, artistic |
| E-commerce Product | High-Key | Clean, consistent, detailed |
| Luxury/Premium | Rim + Gradient Background | Separation, premium feel |

## Light Modifier Quick Guide

| Modifier | Effect | Best For |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Softbox (large) | Very soft, even | Portraits, products |
| Softbox (small) | Soft but defined | Products, controlled soft |
| Beauty Dish | Soft center, defined edge | Beauty, fashion portraits |
| Umbrella | Very soft, spread | General portraits, events |
| Strip Box | Long narrow highlight | Fashion, bottles |
| Ring Light | Shadowless, catch lights | Beauty, social media |
| Reflector | Fill shadows, bounce | Outdoor portraits, product |
| Scrim/Diffuser | Soften harsh light | Outdoor, window control |
| Grid/Snoot | Controlled spot | Hair light, accents |
| Fresnel | Hard, focusable | Dramatic, cinematic |
