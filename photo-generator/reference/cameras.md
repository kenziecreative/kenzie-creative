# Camera Bodies Reference

This file defines the camera bodies available for Nano Banana Pro simulation. Each camera has specific strengths that tell the AI which color science and dynamic range to emulate.

---

## Sony Alpha Series

### Sony A7R V
- **Resolution:** 61MP
- **Best For:** Studio, Product, Landscape, Architecture
- **Strengths:** Micro-textures, extreme detail, high resolution output
- **Color Science:** Neutral, accurate, highly detailed
- **Use When:** You need maximum sharpness and detail; product photography where texture matters; landscape with fine details

### Sony A1 II
- **Resolution:** 50.1MP
- **Best For:** Portraits, Weddings, Fashion, Events
- **Strengths:** Balanced skin tones, handles complex lighting, versatile
- **Color Science:** Warm, flattering skin tones
- **Use When:** Photographing people; mixed lighting situations; need flexibility

### Sony A7S III
- **Resolution:** 12.1MP (optimized for low light)
- **Best For:** Low Light, Night Photography, Moody/Cinematic, Video Stills
- **Strengths:** Clean shadows, handles artificial/neon light without noise
- **Color Science:** Rich shadows, excellent highlight retention
- **Use When:** Night scenes; indoor with poor lighting; moody atmospheric shots

### Sony A7C II
- **Resolution:** 33MP
- **Best For:** Street, Candid, Lifestyle, Travel
- **Strengths:** Organic, approachable professional look
- **Color Science:** Natural, documentary-style
- **Use When:** Casual professional shots; lifestyle content; travel photography

## Hasselblad (Medium Format)

- **Strengths:** Extraordinary detail, medium format depth, legendary color

- **Use When:** Luxury products; editorial fashion; images intended for large prints

- **Resolution:** 100MP
- **Best For:** Portraits, Fashion, Studio

- **Use When:** High-end portraits; beauty photography; fashion editorials

## Film & Cinema

For analog character or motion-picture rendering that digital bodies can't name.

### Pentax 67
- **Format:** Medium format film (6×7)
- **Best For:** Portraits, Fashion, Fine Art with analog soul
- **Strengths:** Legendary shallow-depth medium format look, organic grain
- **Color Science:** Depends on stock — pair with a film stock in post-processing
- **Use When:** You want the analog portrait look (the "Pentax 67 + Portra" aesthetic)

### Contax T2
- **Format:** 35mm film compact
- **Best For:** Candid, Street, Party/Editorial snapshots
- **Strengths:** The iconic point-and-shoot flash look, imperfect and intimate
- **Color Science:** Punchy, slightly warm, flash-forward
- **Use When:** Deliberately casual editorial; 90s/Y2K snapshot aesthetic

### ARRI Alexa 35
- **Format:** Digital cinema (Super 35)
- **Best For:** Cinematic stills, Moody narrative scenes, Brand films
- **Strengths:** Unmatched highlight rolloff, filmic motion-picture color
- **Color Science:** ARRI Reveal — soft, cinematic, wide dynamic range
- **Use When:** A frame that reads as a movie still rather than a photograph
