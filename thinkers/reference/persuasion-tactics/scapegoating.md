# Scapegoating

> Assigning broad blame to a target so a group can avoid complexity, responsibility, or uncertainty.

## Description

Scapegoating persuades by turning a complex problem into a simple villain story. One person, group, department, class, outsider, or identity is made to carry the blame for broader conditions.

It creates emotional relief: now people know who to be angry at. But that relief often comes at the cost of truth, fairness, and repair.

## Mechanism

Scapegoating channels anxiety and anger toward a visible target. It strengthens in-group cohesion by creating an out-group or disposable insider.

It also protects systems and leaders from scrutiny. If the scapegoat is the cause, the structure does not need to change.

## Where It Shows Up

- Politics that blames a group for economic or social complexity.
- Workplaces that blame one person for systemic failure.
- Families where one member is treated as the source of all conflict.
- Debates that assign motive or guilt instead of analyzing causes.

## When It Persuades Legitimately

Accountability is legitimate when it is specific, evidenced, proportional, and connected to actual responsibility.

The healthy version asks: who did what, under what conditions, with what authority, and what system allowed it?

## When It Becomes Manipulative

It becomes manipulative when blame is broader than evidence, when a target is chosen for convenience, or when complexity is suppressed to preserve group unity.

Because scapegoating often targets less powerful people or outsiders, it carries high ethical risk.

## Recognition Cues

- One target is blamed for many unrelated problems.
- Systemic causes disappear from the story.
- Anger is encouraged more than evidence.
- Defending complexity is framed as defending the scapegoat.

## How to Respond

Ask for causal evidence, alternative causes, system conditions, and proportional responsibility. Separate accountability from symbolic blame.

Protect the possibility that more than one thing can be true.

## Decision Prompt

Who or what is being protected by making this target carry the whole explanation?
