Run the **monthly** KR review.

Use the `goal-setting-review` skill for the **monthly** cadence and follow its steps exactly.
For each active Objective it assesses on-track / off-track, classifies why (goal wrong,
execution wrong, or both — the diagnostic the five-minute pulse can't make), records the
specific change being made, and writes the assessment to `goals/journal.md`.

About an hour. Most owners default to blaming execution when the goal is actually wrong; this
review is the chance to catch that.
