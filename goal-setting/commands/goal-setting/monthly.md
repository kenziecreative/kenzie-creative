Run the **monthly** KR review.

Use the `goal-setting-review` skill for the **monthly** cadence and follow its steps exactly.
For each active Objective it assesses on-track / off-track, works the seven-way differential
in order (insufficient dose, insufficient time, mechanism wrong, metric wrong, goal wrong,
external, unknown — the diagnostic the five-minute pulse can't make), checks the
countermetric, sweeps every mitigation trigger, records the specific change being made — any
revision preserves the original commitment in a revision record — and writes the assessment
to `goals/journal.md`.

About an hour. Most owners jump straight to "I didn't work hard enough" — or quietly lower
the target; this review makes them walk past dose, time, mechanism, and metric first.
