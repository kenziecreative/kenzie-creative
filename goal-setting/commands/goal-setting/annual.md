Run the **annual** vision check.

Use the `goal-setting-review` skill for the **annual** cadence and follow its steps exactly.
It reopens the Stage 1 questions — *is this still the right game? is the Horizon 3 vision still
true?* — and either confirms the existing vision or captures a revised Horizon 3 vision. Writes
the annual entry to `goals/journal.md`.

If the vision changed, the skill explicitly hands off: re-run `/goal-setting:horizons` and the
downstream Setup stages, because every stage cascades from a revised Horizon 3. This is where
the Ongoing Arc loops back into the Setup Arc.
