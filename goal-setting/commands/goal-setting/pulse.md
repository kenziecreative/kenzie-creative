Run the **weekly pulse** check.

Use the `goal-setting-pulse` skill for the **weekly** cadence and follow its steps exactly.
It asks the single question — *"Last week, did you execute your systems? If yes, are you
seeing progress in your Key Results? If no, what needs to change?"* — records the boolean
answers and any "what needs to change" note in `goals/journal.md`, and surfaces the
classification logic (executed but not progressing → the goal may be wrong; didn't execute →
the system may be wrong).

Five minutes, no more. It's intelligent adaptation, not a guilt ritual.
