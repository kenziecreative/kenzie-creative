Run the **weekly pulse** check.

Use the `goal-setting-pulse` skill for the **weekly** cadence and follow its steps exactly.
It asks one question per active goal — *did its system run last week? is the KR moving?* —
records the answers per Objective in `goals/journal.md` (an unanswered half gets an explicit
*unknown*, never an inference), sweeps any weekly mitigation triggers, and surfaces the
classification logic (executed but not progressing → the goal may be wrong; didn't execute →
the system may be wrong). During a restart it also evaluates the hold and moves the Restart
Protocol toward its exit.

Five minutes, no more. It's intelligent adaptation, not a guilt ritual.
