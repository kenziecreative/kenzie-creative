Pressure-test the current goal setup.

Use the `goal-setting-pressure-test` skill and follow its steps. It reads the relevant state
(Objectives, KRs, Systems, Mitigations, and the vision they should serve), dispatches the
`goal-setting-critic` agent to red-team the *formulations* — Objective vagueness, KR drift,
systems that are really hopes, incomplete mitigations, anchor mismatch, constraint violations,
and cross-stage contradictions — and returns the findings.

It tests whether your goals are well-*formed* and methodology-faithful, not whether they're the
*right* goals to chase. Acting on the findings is your call, made back in the relevant stage.
