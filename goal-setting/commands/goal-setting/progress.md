Show the Goal Setting dashboard.

Use the `goal-setting-progress` skill and follow its steps. It reads `goals/STATE.md`,
`goals/scorecard.md`, `goals/active.md`, and the latest entries in `goals/journal.md` and
reports the current period, loop position (Setup stage or Ongoing cadence), active anchor
areas with scores, active Objectives with KR status, active systems, outstanding mitigations,
the cadence calendar (last-run dates), and the recommended next action.

Read-only — it writes nothing.
